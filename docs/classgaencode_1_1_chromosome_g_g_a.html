template<class T_CLUSTERIDX, class T_METRIC>
class gaencode::ChromosomeGGA< T_CLUSTERIDX, T_METRIC >

Chromosome encoding two parts.

The encoding is carried out by separating each individual two parts: c = [l|g], the first part is the element section, whereas the second part is called the group section of the individual [1]
